Kryptokrona webminer

Uses your computers CPU to mine Kryptokrona using the cn-pico algo.
You can check your mining status by pasting your SEKR adress at the pool site.

Guide:

Make sure to turn off content blockers to allow the miner to start.
Paste your SEKR adress, you can generate a new adress using our paper wallet.
To mine Kryptokrona connect to pool.kryptokrona.se, port 3333, password: x
4-8 threads and 100% speed should do to start mining!
Remember to keep your computer cool when mining.
